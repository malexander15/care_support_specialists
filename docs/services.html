Our Services

Medicare Benefit Navigation

Understanding Medicare coverage during transitions of care can be challenging, especially when determining what services are available after hospitalization or rehabilitation.

"We help families better understand how Medicare applies to their situation so they can make informed decisions."

Services may include:

  • Explanation of Medicare-covered services
  • Guidance on skilled nursing and rehabilitation coverage
  • Understanding home health eligibility
  • Clarification of timelines and limitations
  • General education to help families make informed care decisions

Medicaid Long-Term Care Application Support

Applying for Medicaid long-term care involves strict documentation requirements and eligibility guidelines. Many families feel overwhelmed by the process.

Support may include:

  • Preliminary eligibility discussion
  • Explanation of income and asset considerations
  • Document checklist and organization support
  • Assistance preparing application information
  • Guidance on common delays and how to avoid them
  • Follow-up coordination throughout the review process

Concierge Care Services

Our concierge care coordination service provides ongoing, hands-on support for families who need consistent assistance managing care.

This service is ideal for families who:

  • Live out of town
  • Have demanding schedules
  • Need ongoing coordination support

Services may include:

  • Appointment coordination
  • Communication support
  • Transportation assistance
  • Organizing care plans
  • Ongoing check-ins

Senior Care Transitions & Placement Guidance

Choosing the appropriate level of care can feel overwhelming, especially under time pressure. We help families understand their options and coordinate next steps.

Support may include:

  • Discharge planning guidance
  • Assisted living and care option exploration
  • Coordination of placement transitions
  • Communication support with facilities
  • Guidance through decision-making process
