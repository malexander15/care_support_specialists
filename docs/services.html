Our Services

Medicare Benefit Navigation

Understanding Medicare coverage can be confusing, especially when families are trying to determine what services are available after a hospital or rehabilitation stay.

Services may include:

  • Guidance regarding Medicare-covered services
  • Understanding rehabilitation and skilled care coverage
  • Assistance navigating home health services
  • Clarification of Medicare care options

Medicaid Long-Term Care Application Support

Applying for Medicaid long-term care can be overwhelming due to documentation requirements and eligibility rules. We assist families with organizing the necessary information and navigating the application process.

Support may include:

  • Initial eligibility review
  • Guidance on Medicaid income and asset requirements
  • Document organization and preparation
  • Assistance preparing the Medicaid application
  • Follow-up coordination during the review process

Concierge Care Services

Families often need support managing multiple healthcare services, appointments, and care providers. Our concierge care coordination services help ensure important healthcare needs are properly organized.

Services may include:

  • Coordination of care services
  • Appointment coordination
  • Communication with healthcare providers when appropriate
  • Assistance organizing care plans
  • Ongoing care coordination support

Senior Care Transitions & Placement Guidance

Choosing the appropriate level of care for a loved one can be challenging. Care Support Specialists LLC assists families with exploring care options and coordinating transitions when moving from hospital or rehabilitation settings to the next level of care.

Support may include:

  • Discharge planning guidance
  • Assisted living placement guidance
  • Care transition coordination
  • Support navigating senior care options
