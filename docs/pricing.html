Pricing

Care Support Specialists LLC offers services based on the level of support needed. Because every situation is unique, services are not one-size-fits-all.

Call Us Email Us

We Provide Both:

  • One-time consultation services
  • Ongoing care coordination support
  • Project-based assistance (such as Medicaid application support)

Pricing is determined based on:

  • The complexity of the situation
  • The level of coordination required
  • The timeframe and urgency
  • The type of services requested

During your initial consultation, we will review your needs and provide a clear outline of recommended services and associated costs.

Our goal is to provide transparent, fair pricing while ensuring you receive the level of support needed during this process.

Have Questions About Our Services or Pricing?

We understand that every family's situation is unique. If you have questions about our services or want to discuss your specific needs, please don't hesitate to reach out.

Regarding the virtual or in person, all services will be virtual but the Medicaid LTC can be done in person by request in Palm beach county
