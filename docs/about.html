About Care Support Specialists LLC

Care Support Specialists LLC was founded to help families navigate the healthcare system during times of transition.

Our Mission

Many families face difficult decisions when a loved one is discharged from a hospital or rehabilitation facility or when long-term care planning becomes necessary.

With experience in hospital discharge planning and care coordination, Care Support Specialists LLC provides guidance and support so families can better understand their options and make informed decisions regarding their loved one’s care.

Our goal is to simplify the process, provide clarity, and help families coordinate the services needed for their loved ones.

Why Families Choose Us

  • Compassionate, family-centered guidance
  • Experience with care coordination and discharge planning
  • Support understanding Medicare and Medicaid-related processes
  • Clear communication during stressful transitions
